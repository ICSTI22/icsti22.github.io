About

The 2nd International Conference on Science Technology & Innovation-Maejo University (ICSTI-MJU) will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results in theory, methodology and applications of Science Technology and Innovation. The conference looks for significant contributions to all major fields of Innovations in Science and Technology, Biological Science and Technology, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer, Data Science and related fields. The aim of the Conference is to provide a platform to the researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and share cutting-edge development in the field.
